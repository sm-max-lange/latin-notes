English
---------

A verb is a word for an action or can be used as a link.

Verbs have the following properties:
* Person
    * 1st (I), 2nd (you), 3rd (they)
* Number
    * Singluar
    * Plural
* Tense
    * Past
    * Present
    * Future
* Voice
    * Active
    * Pasive
* Mood
    * Indicative
    * Imparitive
    * Inq
